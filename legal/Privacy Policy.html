Rivers.run Privacy Policy

Information we Collect or Store

Rivers.run does not directly store any user information, although network request headers sent by your device may be temporarily stored by logging software.

Third Parties

Rivers.run uses Firebase to store login details and your favorite rivers (if you choose to create an account), as well as for analytics purposes.

Firebase typically collects information like pages viewed, time spent in the app, and device IDs to determine unique users.

Rivers.run does not sell this information or share it with any other parties.

Data Deletion

Deleting your rivers.run account will delete your saved login details & favorite rivers. For deletion of other information please contact support@rivers.run.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes become effective 30 days after posting of an updated version of this policy to the Website. Continued use of the Website after any such changes become effective shall constitute your consent to such changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us. You can send us an email at support@rivers.run

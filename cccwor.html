CCC Week of Rivers

On-line river information is available on rivers.run.

  • Rivers Commonly Paddled (tag = CCCWOR)
  • Rivers within 2 hours (tag = CCCWORC)
  • Rivers within 1 hour (tag = CCCWORC1)

Follow the links above to rivers.run and then click on River, Skill, Rating, or Flow Info to sort. You can also click on the river to see the actual flow graphs, to get more information, and to links to AW.

Once on rivers.run, just start typing a tag (ie CCCWOR) in the search bar and the list will automatically sort.

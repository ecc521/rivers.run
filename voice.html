Rivers.run Voice Assistant

Get river levels and more using your voice!

For Google Assistant:

Take a look at the rivers.run action for Google Assistant. It is able to provide water levels, relative flows, and temperatures

For Amazon Alexa:

Take a look at the Rivers.run Water Levels skill for Amazon Alexa. It can provide water levels, relative flows, and temperatures.

For iOS Siri:

You can install Google Assistant for iOS, and use our Google Assistant action.
